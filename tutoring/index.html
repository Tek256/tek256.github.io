Tutoring

Looking to get started in C? Maybe you want to learn programming from nothing! I offer tutoring & mentoring services open to everyone!

Tutoring Tutoring is where I’ll prepare materials beforehand and help teach & solve problems in real time over a voice / video call.

Here are the languages I can help teach:

  • C
  • C++
  • Java
  • C#
  • PHP
  • HTML / CSS / Javascript

As well I can help tutor in Computer Science theory & fundamentals!

Mentoring Mentoring is long form communication where I help guide & answer questions over email. This is most effectively paired with at least semi-regular tutoring sessions. Please keep in mind that mentorship isn’t meant to have an instant response!

About the services:

You’re welcome to record your sessions, I ask that you please don’t post them publicly!

All the materials from a tutoring session will be made available for you to download!

All services come with some lead up info to better tailor everything to you as an individual.

There isn’t a hard timer on services, leeway is expected. Sometimes tutoring might end a little before or after an hour exactly.

You’re encouraged to ask questions, if you don’t understand something, let me know! I want to help learn, not just talk at you!
